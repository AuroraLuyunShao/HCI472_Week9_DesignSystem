Typography

Basic Sans & Sofia Pro

1.250 - Major Third Scale
Base size: 16px

Basic Sans is a sans-serif typeface that features Grotesque features with a functional, neutral and seemingly clean style that looks to keep a neutral appearance on paper, but including lots of details that give it a unique personality. It is especially well-for branding and headlines.

Sofia Pro is a geometric sans font family who dares the modernism and the harmony of the curves. It has very rounded curves with very open terminals that expresses elegant, friendly and contemporary. Sofia Pro has been designed with a higher x-height than other fonts in its class to make tiny readability more obvious in any use situation. It will be ideal for use in small sizes such as business cards or mobile applications.

Basic Sans and Sofia Pro complement each other and successfully expresses my brand attributes of Reliable and Friendly.
